About Us

Innovatics is a place where innovation blends analytics to unveil never thought before business growth opportunities.

We aim to build digital solutions that are personalized and driven by innovation, modern technology, and business intelligence. We distinguish each business and treat them distinctively because each business, its objectives, and its vision are different.

Our approach of nurturing the uniqueness of a business and empowering it with the trending technologies such as artificial intelligence, Machine Learning, Analytics, Internet of Things, etc. aid us to devise a tailored solution for each client. Our holistic services, meticulous process, and agile scrum framework aid our clients to leverage real business value.

Our leaders have rich experience of working with a lean startup to Fortune 500 companies. Some of the names our leaders have benefited with their adroitness are PayTm, Goibibo, Titan, Aditya Birla, Talk Talk, Convergys, and the list goes on.

Our motto is simple: Keep customers and teams at the heart to build a victorious ambiance.
